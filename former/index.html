JEREMY COHEN

Welcome to my personal page ! I am an Artificial Intelligence and Self-Driving Cars Engineer.

Who Am I ?

I am three things : an Engineer in Internet of Things, Self-Driving Cars and Artificial Intelligence; a continuous learner and a community builder (School of AI)

About me

In May 2017, I enrolled in the Udacity Self-Driving Car Engineer Nanodegree Program. From there, I decided that I wanted to work on the most cutting-edge projects we can have to make innovation a reality. I work at ISFM (Intelligent Systems for Mobility) as a self-driving car engineer and I am building an AI community in Paris called the Paris School of AI.

SELF-DRIVING CAR PROJECTS

The projects I built with Udacity lead me to write blogs and to get a job in the field.

These projects are about the four key elements of a self-driving car : Perception, Localization, Planning and Control.
